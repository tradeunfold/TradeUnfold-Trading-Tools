What is a Pip? Forex Price Essentials

In currency trading, a Pip (Percentage in Point) tracks the smallest price fluctuation. Understanding pips is vital for calculating profits and managing risk. Most majors move in 0.0001 increments, while JPY pairs and Metals use 0.01.

Mastering Fibonacci Levels

Fibonacci retracements and extensions are self-fulfilling technical indicators used by millions of traders. Use Retracements (0.382, 0.618) to "buy the dip" and Extensions (1.618) for profit targets.
